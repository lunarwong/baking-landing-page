About Us

Sweet & Simple is a small, family-run bakery located in the heart of our town. We believe that the best desserts are made from scratch, with real ingredients and a whole lot of love.

Our journey started in our home kitchen, where we baked for friends and family. Now, we're sharing our passion with the whole community — bringing you cookies, cupcakes, and muffins that taste just like home.
